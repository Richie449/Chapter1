How To Make a Pizza

Preheat your oven to 425 degrees.

Remove plastic wrap from pizza.

On the top rack in your oven, bake the pizza on the baking tray for approximately 8 minutes. Then rotate the pizza 180 degrees for a more even bake.

Continue to bake the pizza for 4-7 minutes (approximately 12-15 minutes total) until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbling.

Enjoy with your loved ones!
